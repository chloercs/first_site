for a more sociable school

policies

  • Social scene.

    An improvement in the community feel of the School is my main priority. Having events, meeting other students and getting to know professors in a relaxed setting will have a great impact on class time - knowing your peers makes discussion and learning come so much easier!

  • Expanding on Charlotte's good work.

    The current English President has put some great things in place which I would sustain and expand on, such as the useful career events with outside speakers. This could work on suggestions from you - who do you want to hear speak about life after an English degree?

  • A great link between the School and students.

    I would continue to play a very active role on the Facebook groups, be easily contactable through email, and strive to get the answers to any questions or concerns you may have. Charlotte's been great at this, and I wouldn't let it slip.

why chloe?

I am fully committed to being an active and visible President, working to improve the School in every way I can. My policies are designed with the School and its current state in mind, so it can move forward and improve in the ways it needs to.
